  • These steel crossbow bolt were first created by the Jaggenstrike Clan during the {@book Scattered War|TDCSR|1|The Scattered War}, and the secret to crafting them remains well guarded by the {@book Houses of Kraghammer|TDCSR|2|Houses of Kraghammer}. Cataclysm bolts are usually kept in sets of ten, though anyone who holds even one can feel it thrumming with magical power. When you hit with an attack using a cataclysm bolt, the attack deals normal damage. Then roll a {@dice d6} on the following table to determine its additional effect.
