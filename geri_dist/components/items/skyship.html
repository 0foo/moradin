  • Purchasing a skyship costs 100,000 gp, and requires a personal connection with the Alsfarin Union in {@book Ank'Harel|TDCSR|3|Marquet}. A skyship travels at a top speed of 10 miles per hour, can carry total cargo of 10 tons, and requires a crew of six to ten humanoids to operate at peak performance. Most skyship have maximum accommodations for up to 30 passengers. Most skyship only travel between existing skyports (see "{@book Emon Skyport|TDCSR|3|8. Emon Skyport},"), and charge a fee of 2 gp per mile for a passenger ticket. Chartering a skyship to a destination off the standard sky routes costs 15 gp per mile, if the captain is available and willing to undertake the journey. Shipping cargo costs 1 gp per mile per 100 pounds of cargo along normal sky routes, and twice that to other destinations.
