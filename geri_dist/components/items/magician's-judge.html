  • An executioner's blade from the {@book Age of Arcanum|TDCSR|1|Age of Arcanum}, this magic greatsword is one of many similar weapons once used to execute magic-wielding criminals.   • If you hit a creature with an attack using this weapon, you can cast the {@spell dispel magic} spell from the sword against the target as part of the attack. Alternatively, you can use an action to cast {@spell dispel magic} from the sword against a target of your choice. If you need to make a check with your spellcasting ability modifier as part of casting the spell, you make this check with a +3 modifier or your own spellcasting ability modifier, whichever is higher.
