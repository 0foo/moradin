  • You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. When you hit with it, the target takes an extra {@damage 2d6} fire damage.
  • Fire Mastery

  • Dance of the All-Consuming Fire
    • While inside a fire node, you can perform a ritual called the Dance of the All-Consuming Fire, using Tinderstrike to create a devastation orb of fire. Once you perform the ritual, Tinderstrike can't be used to perform the ritual again until the next dawn.
